## Editing The Contact Form

To create a new Contact Form in Strapi, follow these steps:
<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Content Manager">
    Access the Content Manager in Strapi by logging in to your Strapi CMS account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Content M">
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Contact Form">
    Find and click on "ContactForm" in the list of content types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Chaging data">
    Fill or edit in the following details for your new Contact Form:
    - **Title**: Enter the title for your contact page.
    - **Slug URL**: Set the URL that needs to be accessed for your contact page.
    - **Email Field Placeholder**: Define the placeholder text for the email field in the form.
    - **Subject Field Placeholder**: Set the placeholder text for the subject field in the form.
    - **Description Field Placeholder**: Specify the placeholder text for the description field in the form.
    - **Button Text**: Enter the text that will be displayed on the button when submitting the form.
    - **Alternative Text**: This text separates the contact form from the schedule hours.
    - **Support Hours Description**: Provide a description for the support team's schedule hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Saving the changes">
    After entering all the information, click the "Save" button to create your new Contact Form.
  </Step>
</Steps>
