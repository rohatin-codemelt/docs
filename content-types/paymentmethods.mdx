# Managing Payment Methods in Strapi CMS

Payment Method is displayed in the footer of the website. They are used to provide information about various payment options on your website, including their names, images, and display order. You can also include additional details if needed, such as descriptions, limits, transfer instructions, and more.

## 1. Accessing Content Manager

To manage Payment Methods, you need to access the Content Manager in Strapi. Here's how you can get there:

1. Log in to your Strapi CMS account.

2. In the dashboard, click on "Content Manager" in the sidebar.

## 2. Creating a New Payment Method

To add a new payment method to your website, follow these steps:

1. In the Content Manager, locate and click on "Payment Methods" in the list of content types.

2. You'll see a list of existing Payment Methods. To create a new one, click the "Add New Payment Method" button.

3. Fill in the following details for your new method:
   - **Name**: Enter the name of the payment method (e.g., "Credit Card," "PayPal").
   - **Image Dark**: Upload an image representing the payment method for dark backgrounds.
   - **Image Light**: Upload an image for light backgrounds.
   - **Position**: Specify the order in which this method should appear (e.g., 1, 2, 3).

4. Optionally, you can add more information using the rich text editors for various attributes. You can provide details about the payment method, deposit information, withdrawal details, and more.

5. Use the rich text editor to format and style your content:
   - **Content**: In the rich text editor, you can edit text content, change font size, apply text formatting (e.g., bold, italics), and even select text colors. The editor provides a toolbar with various text editing options to make your content visually appealing.

6. After filling in the information, click the "Save" button to create the new Payment Method.

## 3. Editing an Existing Payment Method

If you need to make changes to an existing Payment Method, follow these steps:

1. In the Content Manager, find and click on "Payment Methods" to see the list of existing methods.

2. Locate the Payment Method you want to edit and click on it.

3. Edit the properties as needed, such as the Name, Images, or Position.

4. You can also edit any of the optional rich text attributes if necessary.

5. Use the rich text editor to format and style your content in the same way as when creating a new Payment Method.

6. Remember to save your changes by clicking the "Save" button.

## 4. Deleting a Payment Method

To remove a Payment Method from your website, follow these steps:

1. In the Content Manager, find and click on "Payment Methods" to see the list of existing methods.

2. Locate the Payment Method you want to delete and click on it.

3. In the item's detail view, find the "Delete" or "Trash" button.

4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Please be aware that deleting a Payment Method is permanent and cannot be undone.
