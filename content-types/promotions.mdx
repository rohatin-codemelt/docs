---
title: Promotions
---

The "Promotions" content type in Strapi CMS allows you to manage and display promotions on your website, including the Promotii page and various sliders throughout your casino web app. Each promotion can have a title, URL slug, description, terms and conditions, category (e.g., Casino, Sport, Tournament), position, and promo codes.
<Warning>
  Please be aware that any changes to this content type will require a website redeploy, which typically takes around 2 to 3 minutes.
</Warning>
<Note>This content type benefits a lot from the Publisher Plugin, scheduling the release date and delete date for promotions [find more here](/commonknowledge#publisher-plugin)</Note>

### How to Create a New Promotion Entry
<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Content Manager">
    Access the Content Manager in Strapi by logging in to your Strapi CMS account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Promotions">
    In the Content Manager, find and click on "Promotions" in the list of content types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Promotion">
    To create a new promotion, click the "Add New Promotion" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the Details">
    Fill or edit in the following details for your new Promotion:
    - **Title**: Enter the title for your promotion.
   - **Slug URL**: Set the URL for your promotion, which determines the web address.
   - **Description**: Use the WYSIWYG editor to add a detailed description of the promotion.
   - **Terms and Conditions**: Similarly, use the WYSIWYG editor to provide terms and conditions for the promotion.
   - **Category**: Select the appropriate category (Casino, Sport, or Tournament) from the available options.
   - **Position**: Specify the order in which this promotion should appear.
   - **Promo Codes**: If your promotion includes promo codes, you can add them here. Provide a title, promo code, and a description for each code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Saving the changes">
    After entering all the information, click the "Save" button to create your new Promotion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### How to Modify an Existing Promotion Entry
<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Content Manager">
    Access the Content Manager in Strapi by logging in to your Strapi CMS account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Promotions">
    In the Content Manager, find and click on "Promotions" in the list of content types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the Promotion to Edit">
    Locate the promotion you want to modify and click on it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the Details">
    Edit in the following details for your new Promotion:
   - **Title**: You can update the title of the promotion.
   - **Slug URL**: Modify the URL if needed.
   - **Description**: Edit the promotion's description.
   - **Terms and Conditions**: Update the terms and conditions.
   - **Category**: Change the category if necessary.
   - **Position**: Modify the position for its display.
   - **Promo Codes**: If applicable, you can add, modify, or remove promo codes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Saving the changes">
    After entering all the information, click the "Save" button to create your new Promotion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### How to Delete a Promotion Entry

<Warning>
  Please be aware that deleting a "Promotions" is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you no longer need the item before proceeding with the deletion.
</Warning>
If you no longer need a Promotions and wish to delete it, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Promotions in the Content Manager">
    In the Content Manager, find and click on "Promotions" in the list of content types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Category to Delete">
    You'll see a list of existing Casino Categories. Locate the category you wish to remove and click on it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete the Category">
    In the category's detail view, look for the "Delete" or "Trash" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    When prompted, confirm the deletion. Keep in mind that deleting a Promotion is permanent and cannot be undone, so ensure that you no longer need the category before confirming the deletion.
  </Step>
</Steps>
<Warning>

</Warning>
