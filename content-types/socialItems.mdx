## Accessing Content Manager

To manage your Social Items, you'll need to access the Content Manager in Strapi. Here's how to get there:

1. Log in to your Strapi CMS account.

2. In the dashboard, click on "Content Manager" in the sidebar.

---

## Creating a New Social Item

To add a new social media icon and link to your website, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Content Manager">
    In the Content Manager, find and click on "Social Items" in the list of content types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Social Item">
    You'll see a list of existing Social Items. To create a new one, click the "Add New Social Item" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Details">
    Fill in the following details for your new Social Item:
    - **Icon**: Upload an image for the social media icon.
    - **Title**: Enter the name of the social media platform (e.g., "Facebook," "Twitter," "Instagram").
    - **URL**: Provide the URL or link to the social media profile.
    - **Position**: Specify the order in which this item should appear on your website (e.g., 1, 2, 3).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Your Changes">
    After filling in the information, click the "Save" button to create the new Social Item.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing an Existing Social Item

If you need to make changes to an existing Social Item, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Content Manager">
    In the Content Manager, find and click on "SocialItem" in the list of content types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate the Item">
    You'll see a list of existing Social Items. Locate the item you want to edit and click on it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Properties">
    Once you've selected the item, you can edit the properties as needed:
    - **Icon**: You can update the image representing the menu item. Upload a new image to replace the existing one, if necessary.
    - **Title**: Edit the text that is displayed alongside the icon. Modify it to reflect the updated menu item information.
    - **URL**: Change the path to a different page if required. Update the path to navigate users to the desired page.
    - **Position**: Modify the order in which this item should appear in the burger menu. Adjust the position value (e.g., from 1 to 2) to rearrange the items.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Your Changes">
    After making your edits, remember to save your changes by clicking the "Save" button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting a Social Item
<Warning>
  Please be aware that deleting a "Social Item" is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you no longer need the item before proceeding with the deletion.
</Warning>

If you need to remove a "Social Item" from your website's footer or sidebar, follow these steps:
<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Content Manager">
    In the Content Manager, find and click on "SocialItem" in the list of content types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Social Item to Delete">
    You'll see a list of existing Social Items. Locate the method you want to delete and click on it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete Social Item">
    In the Payment Method's detail view, find the "Delete" or "Trash" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Confirm the deletion when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>


<Tip>
  The updated content will be reflected in the burger menu right away. If it's not updating, you can perform a hard reload by pressing "Ctrl + Shift + R."
</Tip>

By following these steps, you can easily edit existing Social Items to keep your website's navigation up to date and user-friendly.
