# Casino Categories in Strapi CMS

Casino Categories in Strapi CMS allow you to define and organize game groups for the casino page jumbotron slider. This feature lets you create and manage different types of game categories, enhancing the presentation and navigation of your casino platform. This guide will walk you through the steps to create, modify, and delete Casino Category entries in Strapi CMS.

## Creating a New Casino Category

To add a new Casino Category to the casino page jumbotron slider, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In to Your Strapi CMS Account">
    Access your Strapi CMS account by logging in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access 'Casino Categories' in the Dashboard">
    In the dashboard, navigate to "CasinoCategory" by clicking on it in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Casino Category">
    To add a new Casino Category, click the "Add New Casino Category" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the Casino Category Details">
    Fill in the following details for your new Casino Category:
    - **Title**: Provide a name or title for the category.
    - **Icon**: Upload an image that represents this category.
    - **Position**: Specify the order in which this category should appear in the jumbotron slider.
    - **GameGroupEM**: Enter the identifier received from EveryMatrix for this category.
    - **Slug URL**: Define the URL slug to make it <Tooltip tip="SEO-ready and user friendly urls are short and meaningful">user-friendly and SEO-ready.</Tooltip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Your Casino Category">
    After entering the information, save your new Casino Category by clicking the "Save" button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Modifying an Existing Casino Category

If you need to make changes to an existing Casino Category, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access 'Casino Categories' in the Content Manager">
    In the Content Manager, find and click on "Casino Categories" in the list of content types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Category to Edit">
    You'll see a list of existing Casino Categories. Locate the category you want to modify and click on it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Category Properties">
    Once you've selected the category, you can make the following modifications:
    - **Title**: Update the category's name or title.
    - **Icon**: You have the option to change the image representing this category.
    - **Position**: Modify the order in which this category appears in the jumbotron slider.
    - **GameGroupEM**: Edit the identifier received from EveryMatrix for this category.
    - **Slug URL**: Change the slug for the URL if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Your Changes">
    After making your desired edits, ensure to save your changes by clicking the "Save" button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting a Casino Category
<Warning>
  Please be aware that deleting a "Casino Category" is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you no longer need the item before proceeding with the deletion.
</Warning>
If you no longer need a Casino Category and wish to delete it, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access 'Casino Categories' in the Content Manager">
    In the Content Manager, find and click on "Casino Categories" in the list of content types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Category to Delete">
    You'll see a list of existing Casino Categories. Locate the category you wish to remove and click on it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete the Category">
    In the category's detail view, look for the "Delete" or "Trash" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    When prompted, confirm the deletion. Keep in mind that deleting a Casino Category is permanent and cannot be undone, so ensure that you no longer need the category before confirming the deletion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The updated content will be reflected in the burger menu right away. If it's not updating, you can perform a hard reload by pressing "Ctrl + Shift + R."
</Tip>

By following these steps, you can effectively manage Casino Categories in Strapi CMS and maintain an organized and engaging jumbotron slider for your casino platform.
